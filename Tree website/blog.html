The best trees for Johannesburg gardens

19 January 2020

Here is a selection of the best trees for your Johannesburg garden according to www.groundedlandscaping.co.za. The most polular trees in Johannesburg are:

  • Lavender tree
  • Wild peach
  • False olive
  • Pompon tree
  • White pear

Lavender tree (Hyteropyxis natalensis)

The lavender tree is ideal for small gardens, but they are slow growing.

Wild peach (Kiggelaria africana)

This is a semi-deciduous tree that can grow over 12 meters in ideal conditions.

False olive (Buddleja saligna)

This hardy indigenous tree is one of the fastest growers in Gauteng. It can grow at a pace of 1 - 1.5 meters per year.

Pompon tree (Dais cotinifolia)

The pompon tree is a frost hardy tree that sports beautiful pink flowers in summer. It can grow up to 8 meters in ideal conditions.

White pear (Apodytes dimidiata)

The white pear tree has thick, dark green foliage, making it ideal as a screen tree. It is a medium pace grower. It takes about 8 years to reach a height of 6 - 8 meters.
